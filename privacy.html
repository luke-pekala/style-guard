01 Privacy Policy
Last updated: 7 May 2026

Your documents never leave your browser. StyleGuard Pro processes all text locally using JavaScript. Nothing you type or paste is sent to any server.

What we collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time on page
  • Anonymous event counts (analyses run, fixes applied, exports, theme toggles)
  • Browser type, operating system, and general geographic region (country level)
  • Referring website

We do not collect:

  • Any document content you paste or type
  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • IP addresses (Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymisation)

Cookies

Google Analytics sets anonymous first-party cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish unique visitors. These cookies do not contain personal information.

Third-party services

  • Vercel — hosting and deployment
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage analytics
  • Google Fonts — typography (JetBrains Mono)

Opt out

You can opt out of Google Analytics at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact via ai-engineer.app.
